Fried potatoes

Description

Try making these perfectly crispy on the outside and soft inside potatoes.

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • parsley or thyme

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes, slice them in cubes and cook them in boiling water for ten minutes.
  2. Chop garlic and parsley or thyme and fry them in olive oil until garlic becomes crispy and golden.
  3. Drain potatoes, mix them with olive oil and bake them in the oven preheated to 200 degrees.
  4. Once finished, mix them with garlic chips, season and serve.
